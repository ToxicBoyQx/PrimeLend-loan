Frequently Asked Questions

For a personal loan application, you typically need to submit identity proof (PAN card, Aadhaar), address proof, income proof (salary slips or ITR for last 2 years), and bank statements for the last 6 months. Additional documents may be required based on your profile and loan amount.

At PrimeLend, we strive to provide quick loan approvals. For personal loans, the approval typically happens within 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents. Disbursement usually takes place within 1-3 business days after approval.

While we consider multiple factors for loan approval, a CIBIL score of 700 or above generally increases your chances of approval with better interest rates. However, we also consider applications with lower scores based on other eligibility criteria and may offer loans with slightly higher interest rates.

Yes, you can make prepayments or foreclose your loan before the end of the tenure. However, a nominal prepayment/foreclosure fee may apply as per our terms and conditions. This fee typically ranges from 2-5% of the outstanding loan amount depending on the loan type and tenure completed.

The EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is calculated based on the principal loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. You can use our EMI calculator on the website to get an estimate of your monthly payments before applying for the loan.
